About Caught Up!

Caught Up! is a social website for reviewing TV series mimicking the style and layout of Letterboxd.com. This website is currently for demonstration purposes only. Most "user-generated" content is LLM generated for presentation. Visitors can create accounts and post content but are strongly advised not to use real personal or sensitive information.

Created by kwsni.
